Who We Are

Töten is a collective born from the overlooked corners of Great Britain. We grew up with nothing but music and the will to make something of ourselves. No frontman. No ego. Just unity, sound, and message.

Our goal is simple: to show that ordinary people can create extraordinary things. Come to a show and you’ll understand — you don’t just hear Töten. You feel it.
